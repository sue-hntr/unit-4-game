Collect Your Pollen!

Queen Bee will give you a quota of nectar to collect at the start of the game.

By clicking on a flower, you will collect a specific amount of nectar points to your total score.

Win the game by matching your quota without going over. The nectar value of each flower is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time you start a new round, the Queen will change the nectar value of each flower.
